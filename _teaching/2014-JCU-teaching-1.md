The EMAS Peer Tutoring programme, a student initiative, trained by Learning Support advisers, assists students who need help with English, Mathematics, Accounting and Finance, Statistics and IT. I was part of the EMAS program for 1.5 years as a peer tutor, and have been doing so even after I have graduated from the university.

1. Guided students with assignments or theses that require statistical analyses
1. Guided students with research designs and methodology for their theses or assignments
1. Taught and conducted SPSS workshops for students
1. Facilitated and tutored small revision groups for exam preparation
